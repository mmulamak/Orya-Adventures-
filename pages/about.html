Our Mission

To create transformative travel experiences that connect people with the world's most breathtaking landscapes and cultures.

Since 2002, we've been dedicated to crafting adventures that go beyond typical tourism. Our trips are designed to challenge, inspire, and create lasting memories while maintaining the highest standards of safety and sustainability.

  • Certified sustainable tourism operator
  • 100% local guide employment
  • Carbon offset program for all trips
  • Safety certified guides

"Life-changing adventures with responsible travel at their core."

- National Geographic

Our Journey

2002: Humble Beginnings

Founded by outdoor enthusiasts with just three local hiking tours

2008: First International Trip

Launched our inaugural international adventure to the Canadian Rockies

2012: Sustainability Commitment

Became a certified B Corporation, pledging 1% of revenue to conservation

2018: National Geographic Partnership

Recognized as a National Geographic Unique Lodge of the World

2023: 50,000+ Adventurers

Celebrated serving over 50,000 travelers across six continents
